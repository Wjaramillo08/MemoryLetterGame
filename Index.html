Memory Letter Game

Test your memory skills by tapping letters in alphabetical order before time runs out!

How to Play

Game Objective

Memorize letters shown at the start of each round, then tap them in alphabetical order as they fall down the screen.

Scoring System

  • 1 point for each correctly tapped letter
  • Points are earned immediately when you tap a correct letter
  • Complete rounds quickly for bonus points

Game Structure

The game has 5 levels with increasing difficulty:

Level 1

6 letters

Level 2

7 letters

Level 3

8 letters

Level 4

9 letters

Level 5

10 letters

Each level has 4 rounds with decreasing time limits:

  • Round 1: 10 seconds
  • Round 2: 8 seconds
  • Round 3: 7 seconds
  • Round 4: 6 seconds

Game Elements

Normal bubble - Tap to reveal letter

Correct letter - Stays visible

Wrong letter - Hides again after 0.5s

Pro Tips

Focus on Letter Range

When letters appear, quickly note the lowest and highest letter to know the alphabetical range.

Tap Rhythmically

Develop a steady tapping rhythm to maintain speed without mistakes.

Track Movement

Follow the bubbles as they descend to maintain spatial awareness.

Alphabet Practice

Practice saying the alphabet quickly to improve your recall speed.
